FAQ

Is Christmas light installation seasonal?

Yes. Demand can be concentrated, so scheduling, crews, and materials need planning.

Should removals be priced in?

Yes. Removal time, storage, and callbacks should be part of the quote strategy.

Does this guarantee seasonal profit?

No. It is a rough planning calculator; weather, demand, pricing, and operations may vary.
