FAQ

Do window tint laws matter?

Yes. Legal tint limits vary by location and vehicle window. Check local rules before selling installs.

What costs should tint shops include?

Film, blades, squeegees, software, rent or mobile costs, insurance, ads, warranty work, and training should be considered.

Does this guarantee tinting profit?

No. This is rough planning math. Demand, speed, quality, pricing, and legal rules may vary.
