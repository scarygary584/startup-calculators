Before you use this calculator

Use this vending machine profit calculator to estimate daily gross profit, monthly net profit, location fees, restocking costs, and machine payback.

Change the defaults to match your market, pricing, schedule, and real costs. The result is a planning estimate, not a guarantee.

What this means

The calculator estimates product margin, monthly net profit after fees/restocking, and rough payback time.

Truth-check

Bad locations, theft, expired inventory, repairs, product spoilage, refunds, and low foot traffic can kill the numbers.
