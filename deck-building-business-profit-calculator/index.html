Deck building profit formula

Project revenue = deck square feet × price per square foot.

Project cost = materials + crew labor + permit/design/haul-off costs.

Monthly profit = project profit × projects per month − monthly overhead.

FAQ

What should deck builders include? Include lumber or composite decking, framing, hardware, concrete, railing, stairs, permits, disposal, helpers, and callbacks.

Why use price per square foot? It gives a quick estimate, but complex railings, stairs, height, and demolition can change pricing.

Are results exact? No. This is a rough planning calculator.
