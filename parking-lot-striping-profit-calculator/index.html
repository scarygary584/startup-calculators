Before you use this calculator

Use this parking lot striping profit calculator to estimate line revenue, symbol revenue, paint and material cost, travel, on-site hours, profit before overhead, and profit per hour before quoting a striping job.

Change the defaults to match your market, pricing, schedule, and real costs. The result is a planning estimate, not a guarantee.

What this means

The calculator separates line revenue, symbol revenue, and rough profit before overhead so you can see whether the job is worth the time.

Truth-check

Machine wear, insurance, quote time, weather delays, bad layouts, callbacks, slow payment, and customer acquisition can change the real profit.

Certification / license note

Usually low-barrier, but local business licensing, insurance, property approval, traffic/safety rules, and municipal rules may matter.

FAQ

Is this profit guaranteed?

No. These are rough planning estimates before overhead.

Should quote time count?

Yes. Time spent measuring, bidding, communicating, and collecting payment affects real profit.

Can layouts change the price?

Yes. Faded lots, bad layouts, stencils, arrows, curbs, and traffic control can change the quote.
