How to use this hub

Start with the calculator closest to the service you want to test. Then compare it with related service pages to see whether route density, equipment cost, ticket size, or repeat customers matter more.

Common costs people forget

Insurance, drive time, no-shows, callbacks, disposal, supplies, payment fees, repairs, fuel, seasonal demand, and slow lead flow can all change local service profit.
