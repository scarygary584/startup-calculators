FAQ

What affects photo booth profit most?

Bookings, price per event, staffing, travel distance, reliability, and marketing all affect the math.

Does this include taxes?

No. Add sales tax, income tax, bookkeeping, and payment processing separately.

Is demand guaranteed?

No. Demand depends on market, sales, seasonality, event relationships, and reviews.
