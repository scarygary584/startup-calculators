FAQ

How do I price AI automation setup?

Estimate build hours, testing, documentation, revisions, software, and your required margin. The setup fee should cover the real work, not just the happy-path demo.

What belongs in the monthly retainer?

Monitoring, small fixes, usage costs, reports, workflow changes, and support time can be part of the retainer.

Can automation clients become unprofitable?

Yes. Low retainers can collapse if the client needs constant changes, support, or troubleshooting.
