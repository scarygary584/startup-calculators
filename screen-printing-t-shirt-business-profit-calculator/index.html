How the screen printing calculator works

Revenue per order = shirts per order × price per shirt.

Order cost = blank shirts + ink/supplies + labor cost.

Monthly profit = profit per order × monthly orders − monthly overhead.

FAQ

What costs should be included? Include blank shirts, ink, screens, emulsion, packaging, labor, failed prints, rent, software, marketing, and equipment payments.

Why track profit per shirt? It shows whether your pricing still works when order size, blank cost, or labor time changes.

Are results exact? No. This is a rough planning calculator, not tax, legal, financial, or accounting advice.
