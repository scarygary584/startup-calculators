AI margin is not just software

AI service businesses often have hidden human work: onboarding, prompt changes, testing, client calls, handoffs, troubleshooting, analytics, and support.

Price for usage and support

Retainers should cover software, usage, support time, and profit. A cheap monthly plan can become a tiny haunted house if one client uses far more calls, messages, or support than expected.
