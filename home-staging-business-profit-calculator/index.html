FAQ

What is vacant-home staging?

Temporary furniture, art, rugs, lighting, plants, and décor are installed to present an empty listing, then removed later.

What costs should be included?

Include design, labor, delivery, pickup, storage, truck, insurance, cleaning, damage, marketing, and inventory replacement.

Why include extension fees?

Furniture may remain in a listed home beyond the initial rental period, generating extension revenue while inventory stays unavailable for another project.
