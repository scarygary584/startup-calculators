What this means

Truth-check

Towing demand can be real, but truck payments, insurance, fuel, storage, dispatch fees, slow periods, municipal rules, and customer acquisition may change the math.

Certification / license note

Check local towing permits, commercial insurance, storage rules, police rotation rules, roadside safety requirements, and business registration.

FAQ

Why is towing startup cost high?

Truck cost, insurance, equipment, permits, storage, and working capital can add up quickly.

Does this include all insurance?

No. Commercial coverage varies by location, service type, fleet, and risk profile.

Are calls guaranteed?

No. Call volume depends on contracts, dispatch, marketing, reputation, location, pricing, and operations.
