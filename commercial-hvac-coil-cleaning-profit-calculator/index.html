FAQ

How much can a commercial HVAC coil cleaning job cost?

Pricing can vary by unit size, coil condition, rooftop or indoor access, chemical needs, labor time, travel, safety requirements, and whether the work is part of recurring maintenance.

What affects HVAC coil cleaning profit?

Profit depends on the cleaning price, number of jobs, labor time, chemicals, supplies, travel, equipment cost, insurance, callbacks, access difficulty, and monthly overhead.

How long does commercial coil cleaning take?

Time varies by access, coil condition, number of rooftop units or air handlers, setup, rinsing, safety steps, and cleanup. Labor inputs should be adjusted for the actual type of property.

Is coil cleaning a good add-on service for HVAC companies?

It can be a practical add-on for some HVAC companies because commercial clients often need maintenance, but results depend on licensing boundaries, technician capacity, equipment, insurance, demand, and service quality.

Does this require HVAC licensing?

Rules vary. Check HVAC licensing boundaries, insurance, safety, chemical handling, rooftop access rules, commercial property rules, and local requirements before offering services.
