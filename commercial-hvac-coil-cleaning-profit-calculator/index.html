Plain-English notes

This kind of service may be sold to businesses that already need preventive maintenance. HVAC companies, restaurants, grocery stores, property managers, and commercial clients can all have different access rules, expectations, and approval steps.

FAQ

How do you estimate HVAC coil cleaning profit?

Start with job revenue, labor, supplies, travel, recurring contract value, monthly expenses, and equipment payback.

Why do recurring contracts matter?

Recurring maintenance contracts can make revenue more predictable than one-off jobs, but they still require sales, scheduling, insurance, and reliable service.

Does this require HVAC licensing?

Rules vary. Check licensing boundaries, insurance, safety, chemical handling, commercial property rules, and local requirements before offering services.
