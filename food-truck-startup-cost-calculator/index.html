How much does a food truck cost to start?

The total depends on truck or trailer cost, buildout, equipment, permits, commissary rules, inventory, insurance, and cash cushion.

Should I include a cushion?

Yes. Repairs, permits, slow days, and event gaps can create cash pressure.
