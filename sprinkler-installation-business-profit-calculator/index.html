How this calculator works

Monthly revenue = installation price × completed installations + repair revenue.

Monthly profit = revenue − installation direct costs − repair costs − overhead.

Break-even installations use contribution per installation after materials, labor, trenching, travel, and permits.

Yes, but enter only service revenue the business can realistically support.
