FAQ

Is a paid golf challenge booth legal everywhere?

No. It may be treated as a game of skill, contest, promotion, or regulated prize activity depending on location. Check local rules before collecting money or advertising a prize.

What drives the booth math?

Traffic, price per bucket, repeat attempts, days open, prize payouts, location cost, signage, staffing, insurance, permits, and liability coverage all affect the rough profit estimate.

Does this guarantee profit?

No. This is a rough planning calculator. Traffic, repeat attempts, legal setup, prize risk, taxes, and location quality may vary.
