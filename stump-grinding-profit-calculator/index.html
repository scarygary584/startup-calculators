What this means

The calculator estimates the rough margin on each job and what monthly profit may look like after an equipment payment.

Business idea context

Stump grinding is often discussed as a test-first service business because some operators start by renting equipment, proving demand, and only later buying a grinder. The calculator helps compare job profit, equipment payment, and payback before committing cash.

Truth-check

Machine repairs, teeth replacement, hidden rocks, trailer costs, access problems, cleanup, and customer acquisition can change the numbers quickly.

Certification / license note

Usually low-barrier to permit/certification helpful, but tree work, hauling, insurance, and local rules may matter.

FAQ

Should cleanup be included?

Yes. Grinding and cleanup are different workloads, and cleanup can change the price.

What hurts stump grinding profit?

Rocks, roots, tight access, teeth wear, repairs, hauling, and slow lead flow.

Is this before overhead?

Yes. Add insurance, marketing, taxes, financing, and admin time separately.
