Mobile notary profit formula

Revenue per appointment = notary/signing fee + travel fee charged.

Profit per appointment = revenue − fuel − supplies − platform/referral fee.

Monthly profit = profit per appointment × appointments per month − monthly overhead.

FAQ

What costs should be included? Include commission costs, bond/E&O where applicable, printer, paper, toner, fuel, phone, website, platform fees, and marketing.

Why calculate break-even appointments? It shows how many appointments are needed to cover fixed monthly overhead.

Are results exact? No. This is a rough planning calculator. Notary laws and allowed fees vary by location.
