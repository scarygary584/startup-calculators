Certification / license note

Pest control is often regulated. Check pesticide licensing, applicator rules, chemical storage, insurance, safety, and local business requirements.

FAQ

Why does licensing matter?

Pesticide application and safety rules can affect what services you can legally offer.

Does this include route density?

No. Route density can improve margins but depends on customer location and scheduling.

Is recurring revenue guaranteed?

No. Recurring revenue depends on retention, service quality, pricing, demand, and operations.
