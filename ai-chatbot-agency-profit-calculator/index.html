FAQ

What should I charge for a chatbot?

Many agencies use a setup fee plus monthly retainer. The right price depends on niche value, integrations, support, and usage.

What costs matter most?

Platform costs, AI/message usage, support time, integrations, outreach, and client revisions usually matter most.

Is a chatbot agency recurring revenue?

It can be, but retention depends on whether the chatbot produces value and does not create support chaos.
