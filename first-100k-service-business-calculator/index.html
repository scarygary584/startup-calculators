Before you use this calculator

Use this first $100K service business calculator to estimate jobs needed, leads needed, weeks to goal, close rate, average job value, and rough profit.

Change the defaults to match your market, pricing, schedule, and real costs. The result is a planning estimate, not a guarantee.

Default assumptions

  • Revenue goal$100,000
  • Average job value$500
  • Close rate30%
  • Leads per week10
  • Profit margin40%

What this means

The calculator estimates the jobs and leads needed to reach a rough revenue goal, then applies a planning profit margin.

Truth-check

Revenue is not profit. Ad costs, labor, materials, fuel, insurance, taxes, refunds, and unpaid invoices can shrink the real number.

Certification / license note

Depends on the service. Some are low-barrier, some require permits, and others require state licensing or apprenticeship.

FAQ

Can this predict success?

No. It is only a rough planning tool based on your inputs.

What is close rate?

Close rate is the percent of leads that become paying jobs.

Should ad costs be included?

Yes. Add marketing cost and customer acquisition cost to your real planning.
