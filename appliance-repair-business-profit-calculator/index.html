FAQ

What drives appliance repair profit?

Call volume, close rate, diagnostic fee, repair labor profit, parts markup, callbacks, and route efficiency matter.

Do appliance repair businesses need licenses?

Rules vary. Some work may involve electrical, gas, or refrigerant rules. Check local requirements.

Does this include parts inventory risk?

Only partly. You should separately consider slow-moving parts, returns, warranty claims, and ordering delays.
