FAQ

Who buys grill cleaning?

Homeowners, vacation rentals, lake houses, high-end neighborhoods, and outdoor kitchen owners may need grill cleaning.

What upsells can be included?

Propane tank swaps, grill tools, cedar planks, outdoor fridge cleaning, cooler cleaning, and patio or pressure washing add-ons can be modeled.

Is this seasonal?

Often yes. Demand can rise before warm weather, holidays, lake season, and vacation rental turnover periods.
