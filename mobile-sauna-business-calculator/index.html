FAQ

What drives mobile sauna profit?

Booking volume, rental price, delivery radius, cleaning time, maintenance, and repeat customers all matter.

Does this include financing?

No. Add loan payments, interest, taxes, and depreciation separately.

Do local rules matter?

Yes. Check rules for trailers, fire safety, rentals, events, and insurance.
