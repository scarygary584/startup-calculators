Fence building profit formula

Job revenue = linear feet × price per foot + gate/add-on revenue.

Job cost = material cost per foot × linear feet + crew labor.

Monthly profit = job profit × jobs per month − monthly overhead.

FAQ

What costs should be included? Include posts, panels, pickets, concrete, screws, gates, disposal, crew labor, layout time, fuel, insurance, and callbacks.

Why track price per foot? Fence jobs are often estimated by linear foot, but gates, slopes, demolition, and access can change the final price.

Are results exact? No. This is a rough planning calculator.
