How this calculator works

Monthly revenue = average sale price × jobs or sales per month.

Monthly gross profit = monthly revenue − average cost per job × jobs or sales per month.

Estimated monthly profit = monthly gross profit − monthly expenses.

Break-even jobs = monthly expenses ÷ profit per job.
