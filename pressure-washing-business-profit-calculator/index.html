FAQ

Should I charge by hour or job?

Many operators quote by job, but hourly math helps you avoid low-margin work.

Should equipment wear be included?

Yes. Pumps, hoses, nozzles, surface cleaners, and machines wear out.

Can water rules affect the job?

Yes. Wastewater and chemical handling rules vary by location.
