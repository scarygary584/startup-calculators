FAQ

Does this include storage?

No. Add storage, trailer, cleaning supplies, repairs, and admin time separately.

Can weather change the numbers?

Yes. Rain, wind, heat, and cancellations can affect monthly rentals.

Is insurance optional?

You should treat insurance as important and verify requirements locally.
