How this calculator works

Revenue per job = linear feet × installed price per foot + add-on revenue.

Direct cost = gutter materials + add-on materials + crew labor + travel and supplies.

Monthly profit = job contribution × jobs − monthly overhead.

FAQ

How is revenue calculated?

Average footage times installed price, plus downspout and guard add-ons.

Should machine payments be included?

Yes. Put financing and other recurring expenses into monthly overhead.

Does this replace a job estimate?

No. Measure and scope each property before quoting.
