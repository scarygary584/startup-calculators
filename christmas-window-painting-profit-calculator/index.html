Christmas window painting formula

Seasonal revenue = storefront jobs × average price per storefront.

Profit per job = price − paint/supplies − labor − travel.

Seasonal profit = profit per job × storefront jobs − seasonal marketing cost.

FAQ

What costs should be included? Include paint, brushes, markers, window prep, cleanup, ladders, travel, business cards, flyers, and outreach time.

Why calculate profit per hour? Seasonal jobs have limited weeks, so hourly profit matters as much as total revenue.

Are results exact? No. This is a rough planning calculator.
