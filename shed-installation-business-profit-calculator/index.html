How this calculator works

Monthly revenue = average installed shed price × completed installations.

Direct cost includes the shed, foundation, delivery, labor, site preparation, and permits.

Monthly profit = project contribution × jobs − monthly overhead.

FAQ

What costs belong in a shed installation?

Include shed materials, foundation, delivery, site work, assembly labor, permits, cleanup, and overhead.

Can I use this for both kits and custom sheds?

Yes, after replacing the defaults with the correct materials, labor, and selling price.

Does this check zoning?

No. Local setbacks, size limits, foundations, utilities, and permits must be checked separately.
