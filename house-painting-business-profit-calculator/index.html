House painting profit formula

Job revenue = painted square feet × price per square foot.

Job profit = job revenue − paint/materials − labor − travel and prep costs.

Monthly profit = job profit × jobs per month − monthly overhead.

FAQ

Should prep time be included? Yes. Washing, sanding, caulking, masking, setup, cleanup, and callbacks all affect profit.

Does this work for exterior painting? Yes, but exterior jobs may need extra inputs for ladders, lifts, weather delays, and surface repair.

Are results exact? No. This is a rough planning calculator.
