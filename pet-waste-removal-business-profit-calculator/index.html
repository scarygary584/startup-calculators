Pet waste removal profit formula

Monthly revenue = weekly customers × price per visit × visits per customer per month.

Monthly costs = supplies/disposal + fuel + labor + overhead.

Monthly profit = monthly revenue − monthly costs.

FAQ

What costs should be included? Include bags, buckets, disinfectant, route fuel, disposal, website, insurance, software, labor, and customer churn.

Why use recurring customers? Pet waste removal is mostly a route business, so recurring weekly customers are the main profit driver.

Are results exact? No. This is a rough planning calculator.
