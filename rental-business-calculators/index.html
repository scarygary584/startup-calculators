What rental math should include

Do not stop at booking price. Include delivery, setup, cleaning, storage, repairs, insurance, payment fees, software, labor, no-shows, seasonality, and the number of realistic bookings per month.

Equipment payback matters

A rental item can look profitable per event but still take too long to pay back if bookings are thin. Test both profit per booking and months to recover the purchase cost.
