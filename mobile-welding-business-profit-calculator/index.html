Mobile welding profit formula

Revenue per job = service call fee + hourly welding rate × billable hours.

Profit per job = revenue − consumables − fuel/travel − labor cost.

Monthly profit = profit per job × jobs per month − monthly overhead.

FAQ

What costs should be included? Include welder/generator cost, truck setup, insurance, rods, wire, gas, grinding discs, PPE, fuel, maintenance, licensing, and downtime.

Why include a service call fee? Mobile work loses time to travel, setup, traffic, and jobsite delays. A call fee helps cover that time.

Are results exact? No. This is a rough planning calculator, not financial or licensing advice.
