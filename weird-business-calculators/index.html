Why weird calculators can work

Unusual business ideas can create curiosity, but curiosity is not cash. These calculators help test price, volume, startup cost, labor, and payback before the idea turns into a garage full of equipment.

Use boring math on strange ideas

Every odd business still has normal business math: revenue, costs, labor, marketing, insurance, demand, seasonality, and customer acquisition. The stranger the idea, the more useful the calculator.
