Trade math is not just skill

Skill matters, but so do licensing, insurance, material waste, callbacks, scheduling, estimates, vehicle cost, and the time it takes to win jobs.

Use payback before purchase

Before buying tools, machines, trailers, or a work truck, compare startup cost against realistic monthly profit. A machine without customers is just an expensive pet.
