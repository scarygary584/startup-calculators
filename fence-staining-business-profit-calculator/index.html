FAQ

What affects fence staining profit most?

Square-foot price, prep time, stain cost, labor hours, weather, and callbacks affect profit.

Should prep be priced separately?

Often yes. Cleaning, sanding, masking, and repairs can change labor time and material needs.

Does this include taxes?

No. Monthly profit is before taxes and before any costs you do not enter.
