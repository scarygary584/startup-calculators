FAQ

Do I need permits for a parking lot pizza setup?

Often yes. Food handling, health department, sales tax, fire safety, and local vendor rules may apply.

Can a location partner reduce rent?

Possibly. Some hosts may accept lower or no location fees if the pizza setup brings traffic, but approval is still needed.

What hurts the math?

Weather, low traffic, slow service, food waste, helper labor, permits, and location fees can reduce profit.
