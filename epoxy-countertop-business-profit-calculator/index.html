Epoxy countertop profit formula

Job revenue = countertop square feet × price per square foot.

Job cost = resin/materials + labor + waste or callback reserve.

Monthly profit = job profit × jobs per month − monthly overhead.

FAQ

What costs should be included? Include epoxy, pigments, primer, PPE, sanding discs, masking, shop supplies, travel, labor, waste, and callbacks.

Why include a callback reserve? Epoxy work can have bubbles, dust, adhesion issues, and finish problems. A reserve keeps the estimate more realistic.

Are results exact? No. This is a rough planning calculator.
