FAQ

Why say documentation instead of inspection?

Inspection can trigger licensing requirements in some places. This page uses documentation, photo report, and roof condition documentation because rules vary.

Who might buy roof photo reports?

Roofing companies, insurance-related vendors, property managers, construction companies, solar companies, and home inspectors may be potential customers.

What add-ons could be modeled?

Thermal imaging, construction progress photos, solar documentation, and social media footage can be add-ons if legal and appropriate.
