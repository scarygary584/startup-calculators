FAQ

What affects mobile dog grooming profit most?

Average groom price, dogs per day, route density, cancellation rate, van cost, and repeat clients matter.

Should I include van payment?

Yes. A mobile grooming van or trailer can be a major fixed cost and should be included in planning.

Are grooming licenses required?

Rules vary by location. Check business, animal handling, insurance, wastewater, and mobile service requirements.
