FAQ

What can improve mobile sauna utilization?

Partnerships with gyms, wellness studios, Airbnbs, event hosts, and corporate wellness buyers can help fill more dates.

Should cold plunge costs be included?

Yes. Ice, water handling, cleaning, transport, maintenance, and safety planning should be included in the real math.

Does this guarantee monthly profit?

No. This is a planning estimate. Demand, weather, pricing, maintenance, seasonality, and local rules may vary.
