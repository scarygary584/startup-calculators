Window washing profit formula

Revenue per job = windows per job × price per window.

Profit per job = revenue − supplies − labor − fuel/travel.

Monthly profit = profit per job × jobs per day × work days per month − monthly overhead.

FAQ

What costs should be included? Include squeegees, poles, soap, towels, ladders, insurance, fuel, helpers, callbacks, and lead costs.

Why use profit per hour? It shows whether a route is worth the time after travel and setup.

Are results exact? No. This is a rough planning calculator.
