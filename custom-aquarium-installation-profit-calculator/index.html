How this calculator works

Project revenue = aquarium project price + design fee.

Project cost includes fabrication, cabinetry, filtration, lighting, plumbing, delivery, installation labor, and aquascaping.

Monthly profit adds maintenance revenue, subtracts maintenance direct costs, and subtracts overhead.

FAQ

What belongs in a custom aquarium estimate?

Include every design, fabrication, cabinetry, equipment, plumbing, delivery, labor, aquascaping, overhead, and risk cost.

Why model maintenance separately?

It can be recurring revenue, but it also creates recurring labor, travel, supplies, and service obligations.

Does this include engineering?

No. Add structural, electrical, plumbing, waterproofing, and engineering costs where required.
