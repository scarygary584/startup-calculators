FAQ

What drives awning profit?

Accurate measuring, supplier cost, project price, motorization, crew speed, and low callback rates.

Should motorized jobs be modeled separately?

Yes. Change the motor and controls cost to match the actual job mix.

Does this include structural engineering?

No. Add engineering, lifts, electrical work, and special permits where required.
